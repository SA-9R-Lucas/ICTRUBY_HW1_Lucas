Tangled

Tangled is about a young girl who was kidnapped by an old woman who wanted to remain young.Rapunzel had magic golden hair due to a flower her mother drank to cure the mothers illness and to give birth to Rapunzel properly. Her hair had the power to bring back what was once in the past, in the case of the old lady, her looks while she was young. On Rapunzel's 18th birthday she wished to get out of the tower and then suddenly a thief came into the tower she was trapped in. The thief grew fond of Rapunzel and he and her got out of the tower, at first he only helped her because the crown Rapunzel had was worth a lot. At this time Flynn, the Thief had no idea Rapunzel was the lost princess. In the end he loved her and even died for her after getting rid of the old lady. With Rapunzel's powers he brought him back to life and they got married and lived happily ever after.
